\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Computes one payoff using exact one-\/step sampling (no time grid). 

Let X\+\_\+t = log S\+\_\+t. Under Black–\+Scholes:\+ X\+\_\+T = x0 + (r -\/ 0.\+5 sigma\texorpdfstring{$^\wedge$}{\string^}2) T + sigma sqrt(\+T) z.

Conditional on (X\+\_\+0=x0, X\+\_\+T=x\+\_\+t), the (driftless) Brownian bridge maximum has CDF:\+ P(max X \texorpdfstring{$<$}{<}= m \texorpdfstring{$\vert$}{|} x0, x\+\_\+t) = 1 -\/ exp(-\/2 (m-\/x0)(m-\/x\+\_\+t)/\+(sigma\texorpdfstring{$^\wedge$}{\string^}2 T)), for m \texorpdfstring{$>$}{>}= max(x0, x\+\_\+t).

Inversion yields a quadratic equation in m with two roots:\+
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item the upper root gives the maximum,
\item the lower root gives the minimum (by symmetry). 
\end{DoxyItemize}
