\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxylink{namespace_random}{Random} draws needed for one one-\/step lookback path. 

We separate the {\itshape generation} of randomness from the {\itshape pricing}:\+ the sampler stores samples so that the same random numbers can be reused for price and for Greeks (common random numbers /\+ CRN).
